Wars' Time Cycle

Data Visualization About Wars

This visualization is to show wars in the scope of time through the metaphor of spiral. The spiral shape represents time span from 1820 to 2003, with each dot on the line indicating the beginning of a war. When mouse is hovering a dot, a red line will be drawn, with length representing time and stroke representing casualty. Click button below to see the overview of all the wars.

Data source: The Correlates of War Project
